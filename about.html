About Us

Who We Are

In the year 2016, Cuddalore Community Livelihoods Services Producer Company Limited has been promoted by a collective of 10 people with disabilities and marginalized section who are engaged in small farming and off farm rural produces in Cuddalore District, Tamilnadu. The company has been promoted and established by and for the welfare of the people with disabilities to remove barriers hurdling their socio-economic growth. The company by nature provides permanent and sustainable source to its shareholders through techno-managerial services. The livelihood intervention initiated by the company is an innovative model that would suit all walks of the people, particularly the vulnerable living in rural India with an invitation to all those work towards welfare of the marginalized society.

Vision

Envisage an integrated service model with backward and forward linkages for people with disabilities and other economically marginalized by building social enterprise(s).

Mission

Provide support for establishing sustainable, viable enterprise(s) and provide services across value chains to people with disabilities and other economically marginalised based on the feasibility and towards economic resilience of affected communities
